About me

I am a fifth-year Ph. D. Candidate at the University of Southern California, VGL-ICT, working with Professor Hao Li, Randall Hill, and Yajie Zhao. Previously, I was in the Machine Learning Group in the School of Software, Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Professor Mingsheng Long and Dr. Yue Cao. I spent 12 wonderful weeks as a research assistant in Prof. Kilian Q. Weinberger's lab at Cornell University during the summer of 2017, working with Gao Huang. I am currently interested in fast and accurate geometry deep learning, low-level feature learning for 3D vision, differentiable rendering, digital human (face capturing and rendering), and etc. I am also a 3D digital artist and an active gamer in first-person shooting and real-time strategy games.
