About me

I work at Google. I obtained my Ph. D. from the University of Southern California, VGL-ICT, working with Professor Randall Hill, Yajie Zhao, and Hao Li. Previously, I was in the Machine Learning Group in the School of Software, Tsinghua University, advised by Professor Mingsheng Long and Dr. Yue Cao. I spent 12 wonderful weeks as a research assistant in Prof. Kilian Q. Weinberger's lab at Cornell University during the summer of 2017, working with Dr. Gao Huang.
I am specifically interested in fast and accurate geometry deep learning, low-level feature learning for 3D vision, differentiable rendering, digital human (face capturing and rendering), and etc. I am also a 3D digital artist and a gamer.
